Project 01 · Logistics Operations Analysis

Port-to-Warehouse Container Delivery Lead Time Optimization

Independent supply chain case study analyzing bottlenecks in container delivery from Tanjung Priok to Jakarta warehouses — 60-shipment modeled dataset grounded in public data from BPS and Pelindo.

Identified 3 bottlenecks: port wait (avg 173 min vs 52 normal), warehouse congestion (avg 121 min), peak-hour dispatch (85 vs 58 min non-peak)
Impact simulation projecting on-time delivery from 25% to ~70% with 3 evidence-based recommendations
Full 5-tab workbook: Project Brief, Public Sources, Shipment Data, Analysis, Recommendations
Google SheetsAVERAGEIF · COUNTIF · IFBPS · Pelindo DataImpact Simulation

Project 02 · Industry Research Brief

Bridging the Talent Gap in Indonesia’s Logistics & Maritime Sector

Strategic industry brief on structural human capital challenges facing integrated logistics companies like Samudera Indonesia — with three evidence-based HC recommendations grounded in McKinsey, ManpowerGroup, WEF, and ALI data.

Three HC recommendations: rotational development programs, digital upskilling mandate, retention through mobility and meaning
Frameworks: McKinsey 3-Horizon, Experiential Learning Theory (Kolb), Self-Determination Theory (Deci & Ryan)
