Port-to-Warehouse Container Delivery Lead Time Optimization

Independent supply chain case study analyzing bottlenecks in container delivery from Tanjung Priok to Jakarta warehouses, using a 60-shipment modeled operational dataset grounded in public industry data from BPS and Pelindo.

Identified 3 primary bottlenecks: port waiting time (avg 173 min vs 52 min benchmark), warehouse congestion (avg 121 min), and peak-hour dispatch (85 min vs 58 min non-peak)
Built impact simulation projecting on-time delivery improvement from 25% → ~70% with 3 operational recommendations
Full 5-tab Excel deliverable: Project Brief, Public Sources, Shipment Data, Analysis, Recommendations
